About us

The Bravins Maternity and Rescue Centre will target the vulnerable pregnant girls and women in the society who face challenges in trying to access maternity care for themselves and their newborns. The rescue centre will serve to provide a home for the rescued girls and women and their newborns.

Gallery
KEY SERVICES
Provide maternal care to girls and mothers with unwanted pregnancies. Health care support to pregnant girls/mothers and their new born babies. Guidance and counseling. Provide community awareness on pregnancy, prenatal and postnatal care. Provide literacy and business skills for self-employment.
